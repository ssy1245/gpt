Emily forgot her umbrella before leaving for work. Dark clouds gathered as she walked to the bus stop. Halfway there, it began to rain heavily. She arrived at the office soaked and uncomfortable. Emily spent the morning trying to dry her clothes.

Tom decided to cook dinner for his friends. He followed a recipe he found online. Halfway through, he realized he forgot to buy salt. Tom finished the dish without it anyway. His friends said the food tasted a little bland.

Lily wanted to start jogging every morning. On the first day, she woke up before sunrise. The air was cold and the streets were quiet. She ran shorter than she planned but felt proud. Lily decided to try again the next day.

Mark borrowed his sister’s bike for the afternoon. He rode it to the park near his house. While locking it up, he dropped the key in the grass. After searching for a while, he finally found it. Mark returned the bike home before dinner.

Anna planted tomato seeds in her backyard. She watered them carefully every day. After a few weeks, small green plants appeared. Anna continued taking care of them all summer. By August, she was picking fresh tomatoes.

Jason arrived early at the movie theater. He bought popcorn and chose a seat near the middle. Soon, the theater began to fill with people. The lights dimmed and the movie started. Jason relaxed and focused on the screen.

Nina wanted to surprise her mother with breakfast. She quietly went into the kitchen that morning. While cooking, she accidentally burned the toast.
Nina made a new batch more carefully. Her mother smiled when breakfast was ready.

Kevin signed up for an online photography class. He watched the first lesson after work. The instructor explained how to use natural light. Kevin tried taking photos near his window. The pictures looked better than before.

Sara lost her phone while shopping downtown. She retraced her steps through the stores. At the last shop, an employee recognized it. The phone had been kept behind the counter. Sara thanked the employee and left relieved.
